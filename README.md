# Digital Marketing Internship Project

## Project Title
Social Media Marketing Strategy and Brand Engagement Analysis

## Description
This project focuses on analyzing the digital marketing strategies used by a brand to improve its online presence and audience engagement. The study examines social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube to understand content marketing and promotional techniques used by the brand.

## Objectives
- To understand the concept of digital marketing
- To analyze social media marketing strategies
- To study audience engagement techniques
- To suggest improvements for digital marketing strategies

## Tools and Platforms Used
- Social Media Platforms
- Digital Marketing Analysis
- Content Marketing Strategy

## Project Components
- Internship Report
- Social Media Analysis Screenshots
- Marketing Strategy Evaluation
